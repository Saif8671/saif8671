## 👨‍💻 About Me

> *"I didn't choose cybersecurity. I chose to understand how things break — and how to stop them."*

I'm **Saif ur Rahman**, a self-driven cybersecurity and ML enthusiast on a mission to become a **SOC Analyst** who doesn't just respond to threats — but anticipates them.

My journey started with curiosity: *Why do systems fail? How do attackers think? Can AI make defenders smarter?*
That curiosity turned into late nights with Kali Linux, packet captures in Wireshark, and training ML models to detect what humans miss.

Today I'm building at the intersection of **security engineering and artificial intelligence** — crafting tools like phishing detectors, intrusion detection systems, and cryptographic implementations that solve real problems.

I'm not waiting to land a SOC role to start thinking like an analyst.
I'm doing it now — one project, one CTF, one commit at a time.

**What drives me:**
- 🔐 Making systems harder to break
- 🤖 Using ML to automate threat detection
- 🛠️ Building things that actually work in the real world
- 📈 Growing in public — every repo is a learning log
